About TAKEDI

Delivering premium industrial branding solutions with over 15 years of experience in the mining and construction sectors.

Crafting Visual Excellence Since 2011

Founded in Mokopane, Limpopo, TAKEDI has grown from a small print shop to a leading industrial branding solutions provider. We specialize in large-format printing, vehicle branding, and PPE supply for the mining and construction industries.

Our commitment to quality, durability, and compliance has made us the trusted partner for major industrial operations across South Africa. We understand the harsh conditions your brand needs to withstand, and we deliver solutions that last.
